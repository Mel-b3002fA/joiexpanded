Joi began as a passion project rooted in a deep fascination with artificial intelligence and human connection. What started as a simple chatbot quickly evolved into a d ocumented journey of building an AI that feels truly personal

Trained on real, human conversations—specifically, years of my own WhatsApp chats—Joi reflects the tone, nuance, and emotional rhythm of authentic communication.

Every step of Joi's development has been shared openly and transparently on YouTube, from the early concept sketches to training experiments and live demos.

This project isn’t just about creating a chatbot—it’s about exploring the emotional potential of AI and inviting others to follow the process from raw data to real dialogue.
